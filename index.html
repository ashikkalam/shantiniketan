About Shantiniketan

The vision behind Shanthiniketan is the creation a generation of good-natured human beings who are an asset to their families as well as to the society they belong to. In that pursuit, we keep children at the heart of every decision we make.With that vision as the fulcrum, we set on a mission to leave no child behind. This is why at Shanthiniketan, there is no punishments or no competitions, and every child gets the opportunity to participate in every event that happens at the school. We lay emphasis on child centred-education, learning by example and all round development of the child than just the rat race for more marks. The principal Dr Nirmala makes sure through her interaction and talks that this message is made clear to every member of the staff and students. This approach has seen great dividends; typical teenager problems like teasing, bullying, poor- self image are all absent in the children of Shanthiniketan. The environment that is stimulating and stress-free makes sure that children do well in their board examinations and other competitive examinations.
